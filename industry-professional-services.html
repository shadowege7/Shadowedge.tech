Professional services IT FAQs

What kinds of professional services firms do you support?

Consulting practices, accounting firms, engineering and architecture offices, marketing agencies, and other client-facing organizations where billable time, client data, and responsive communication drive the business.

How does managed IT help a billable-hours business?

Downtime in a professional services firm converts directly into lost billable time. Proactive monitoring, fast help desk response, and reliable collaboration tools keep your team producing, and predictable monthly IT costs make budgeting straightforward.

Can you support our collaboration and file-sharing tools?

Yes. We manage Microsoft 365 and Google Workspace environments end to end, including secure external file sharing with clients, retention policies, and permissions hygiene, which is usually where firms accumulate quiet risk.

How do you protect the client data we hold?

With the same layered approach we bring to regulated industries: multifactor authentication, endpoint protection, encrypted backup, and access controls. Client security questionnaires increasingly ask for proof of these controls, and we help you answer them credibly.
