Frequently asked questions

Which cloud platforms does ShadowEdge work with?

We design and support environments built on Microsoft 365, Google Workspace, and Amazon Web Services, and we hold partner relationships with each. The right platform depends on your applications, compliance requirements, and how your team works.

Should we move everything to the cloud or keep some systems on-premises?

It depends on your applications, bandwidth, compliance requirements, and budget. Many businesses land on a hybrid approach, moving email, files, and collaboration to the cloud while keeping specific line-of-business systems local. We assess your environment before recommending a direction.

Can you design the network for a new or relocated office?

Yes. We handle network design for new buildouts and relocations, including structured cabling coordination, firewall and switch configuration, wireless coverage planning, and secure connectivity back to your other locations or cloud resources.

How do you keep cloud environments secure?

Cloud security starts with identity: multifactor authentication, conditional access, and least-privilege permissions. We layer that with configuration hardening, monitoring, and backup, because cloud platforms secure their infrastructure but leave the configuration and data protection to you.
