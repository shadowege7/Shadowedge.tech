What managed IT support covers

Managed IT support is the day-to-day work of keeping your people productive. It brings four things together: a help desk your team can reach when something is not working, monitoring that watches workstations and servers for trouble, patch management that keeps operating systems and applications current, and ongoing oversight of the environment as a whole.

Rather than waiting for something to break, we track the health of your systems and act on early warning signs, such as a failing drive, a server running low on space, or a missed security update.

  • Help desk support for everyday questions and issues
  • Monitoring and maintenance of endpoints and servers
  • Patch and update management across your devices
  • Asset tracking and documentation of your environment

Fully outsourced vs. co-managed IT

Some organizations want us to run technology from end to end. Others already have internal staff and need us to fill specific gaps. We support both models. Fully outsourced support fits businesses without a dedicated IT person, where we become the technology team. Co-managed IT fits companies that have someone in-house but need extra coverage, after-hours monitoring, or specialized help on projects, so your staff can stay focused on the work only they can do. Many clients pair support with our cybersecurity services to keep protection and daily operations under one roof.

How ShadowEdge approaches support

Break-fix support only reacts after something has already gone wrong. Our approach is proactive: we watch for problems, resolve many of them before your team notices, and keep small maintenance tasks from piling up into an outage. That steadier baseline also gives leadership better visibility into what is running and what needs attention.

Onboarding follows a planned transition. We review your environment and documentation, take over monitoring tools and vendor relationships in an orderly handover, and keep support running without a gap. Over time we can layer in IT consulting and vCIO planning so technology decisions line up with where the business is headed.

Frequently asked questions

What is included in managed IT support?

A managed IT support plan typically covers help desk assistance for your team, monitoring and maintenance of workstations and servers, patch management, and ongoing oversight of your technology environment. It replaces the break-fix model with a proactive relationship where issues are caught and resolved before they disrupt work.

How is managed IT support priced?

Most engagements are scoped as a predictable monthly fee based on the number of users and devices supported and the level of coverage your business needs. We review your environment first, then recommend a plan, so you are not paying for services you do not use.

Can you work alongside our existing internal IT staff?

Yes. We support both fully outsourced arrangements and co-managed IT, where we handle day-to-day support, monitoring, or specialized projects while your internal team focuses on business-specific priorities.

How do we switch from our current IT provider to ShadowEdge?

We handle transitions regularly. The process starts with a review of your environment and documentation, followed by a planned handover of credentials, monitoring tools, and vendor relationships, so support continues without interruption for your team.

What size businesses do you support?

We work with small and midsize organizations, and rather than setting a fixed cutoff, we scope each plan to the number of users and devices involved and the systems your business depends on. That keeps coverage right-sized whether you have a single office or several locations.
