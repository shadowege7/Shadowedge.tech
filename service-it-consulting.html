Frequently asked questions

What is a vCIO?

A vCIO, or virtual Chief Information Officer, gives your business executive-level technology leadership on a fractional basis. Instead of hiring a full-time CIO, you get strategic planning, budgeting guidance, and vendor oversight scaled to the size of your organization.

How is IT consulting different from managed IT support?

Managed support keeps your day-to-day technology running. Consulting looks further ahead: where your systems should be in one to three years, what to budget, which investments to prioritize, and how technology decisions support your business goals. Many clients use both together.

Do we really need a technology roadmap?

If technology decisions at your company happen reactively, usually when something breaks, a roadmap changes that. It sequences replacements, upgrades, and security investments over time, which smooths out spending and prevents emergency purchases at premium prices.

How does a consulting engagement start?

It starts with an assessment of your current environment: hardware age, software licensing, security posture, and how well your systems match the way your business actually works. From there we build recommendations and a prioritized plan you can act on.
