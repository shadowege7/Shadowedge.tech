FAQ

Bay Area IT services FAQs

Which parts of the Bay Area does ShadowEdge serve?

We support businesses throughout the San Francisco Bay Area, combining remote-first support for daily needs with scheduled onsite work for projects, hardware, and network buildouts.

Can you support a hybrid or fully remote Bay Area workforce?

Yes. Much of our tooling is built for distributed teams: cloud identity and collaboration platforms, secure remote access, endpoint management for laptops wherever they are, and a help desk your employees can reach from anywhere.

We have offices in both the Bay Area and Sacramento. Can you cover both?

Yes, and that footprint is exactly where ShadowEdge operates. We manage multi-site networks spanning the Bay Area and the Sacramento region under a single agreement with consistent security and support at every location.

Do you work with startups and fast-growing companies?

Yes. Growing companies benefit most from getting identity, security, and device management right early, before scale makes cleanup expensive. Our plans are structured to add users and locations without renegotiating from scratch.
