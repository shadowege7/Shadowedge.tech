Nonprofit IT Support

ShadowEdge provides cost-conscious managed IT and cybersecurity that helps mission-driven organizations do more with limited resources.

Nonprofit organizations often operate with lean staff and tight budgets, but still manage donor data, grant compliance requirements, and program systems that need to stay secure and available. Stretching IT further without adding risk requires a partner who understands both the constraints and the mission. ShadowEdge helps nonprofits build dependable, right-sized technology environments that support their work rather than compete with it for budget.

  • Managed IT support scaled to fit nonprofit budgets and staffing levels
  • Security protections for donor data, financial records, and grant-related systems
  • Guidance on technology budgeting and planning tied to funding cycles
  • Reliable support for remote and distributed program staff
  • Backup and recovery planning to protect donor and program data
