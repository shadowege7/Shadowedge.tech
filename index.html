FAQ

Common questions about working with ShadowEdge

What is a managed IT services provider (MSP)?

An MSP is an outsourced IT partner that proactively monitors, maintains, and supports your technology environment, including help desk support, cybersecurity, networking, and cloud infrastructure, so you don't need a full in-house IT department.

What areas does ShadowEdge serve?

ShadowEdge supports businesses throughout Northern California, including Sacramento, Rocklin, Roseville, Granite Bay, Folsom, Elk Grove, Placer County, and the San Francisco Bay Area.

What industries does ShadowEdge work with?

We support healthcare, finance, legal, professional services, nonprofit, manufacturing, automotive dealership, and education organizations, tailoring our approach to each industry's operational and regulatory needs.

Does ShadowEdge help with cybersecurity and compliance?

Yes. We provide layered cybersecurity defense, monitoring, and policy-based controls, along with compliance support aligned to standards including HIPAA and PCI.

How do I get started with ShadowEdge?

Contact us to schedule a consultation. We'll review your current environment, security priorities, and growth goals, then recommend a support plan that fits your business.
