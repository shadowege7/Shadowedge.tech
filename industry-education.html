Education IT Support

ShadowEdge delivers reliable, budget-conscious IT support and security for schools and educational organizations managing students, staff, and campus systems.

Schools and educational organizations manage a wide range of systems, from student information platforms to staff devices and campus networks, often with limited IT staff and constrained budgets. Protecting student data and keeping systems available for instruction requires a support model built for these constraints rather than a generic approach. ShadowEdge partners with education organizations to provide dependable support and security without straining already limited resources.

  • Managed IT support scaled to fit school and district budgets
  • Security protections for student information and staff data
  • Device management for classroom and administrative technology
  • Reliable network support for instructional and administrative systems
  • Planning and budgeting guidance tied to school funding cycles

FAQ

Education IT FAQs

What types of educational organizations do you work with?

Private schools, charter schools, tutoring and enrichment programs, and other educational organizations that need dependable technology without the budget of a large district IT department.

How do you protect student data?

Student records get encryption, role-based access controls, and tested backups, with account policies that fit how schools actually operate, including staff turnover at semester boundaries and shared classroom devices.

Can you help stretch a limited technology budget?

Yes. Education pricing from Microsoft, Google, and hardware vendors is significant, and we help schools claim it. We also build refresh plans that spread device replacement across budget years instead of forcing large one-time purchases.

Do you manage classroom and student devices?

Yes. We manage fleets of classroom and 1:1 devices with centralized policies for updates, content filtering, and app deployment, so teachers spend class time teaching rather than troubleshooting.
