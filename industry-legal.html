Legal IT Support

ShadowEdge provides confidentiality-focused IT support, secure document workflows, and responsive help desk service for legal teams.

Law firms handle privileged and confidential client information that requires strict access control and airtight document security, often across multiple case management and e-discovery platforms. Attorneys and staff also need IT support that responds quickly, since delays affect billable hours and client deadlines. ShadowEdge works with legal teams to protect sensitive files while keeping systems fast and reliable.

  • Access controls and encryption to protect privileged client documents and communications
  • Secure remote access for attorneys and staff working outside the office
  • Fast, responsive help desk support to minimize disruption to billable work
  • Email security and phishing protection tailored to the risks legal teams face
  • Support for case management, document management, and e-discovery platforms
