Northern California IT services FAQs

Where in Northern California does ShadowEdge provide IT services?

We support businesses across the Greater Sacramento region, including Sacramento, Rocklin, Roseville, Granite Bay, Folsom, and Elk Grove, as well as the San Francisco Bay Area. Remote support extends our reach to teams anywhere in Northern California.

Do you provide onsite support or is everything remote?

Both. Most day-to-day issues are resolved quickly through remote support, and we schedule onsite visits for hardware work, network projects, and situations where hands-on help is genuinely faster.

Can you support a business with offices in multiple Northern California cities?

Yes. Multi-site support is one of our strengths: we design secure connectivity between locations and manage every office under one agreement, so your team gets consistent support no matter which site they work from.
